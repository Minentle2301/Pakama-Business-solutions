Who We Are

Pakama Business Solutions is a black-female owned HR and business management consulting company. We provide comprehensive HR and business management consulting services across diverse industries and sectors within our dynamic economy. With over 22 years, we are seasoned HR practitioners with expertise in various industries including mining, manufacturing, engineering, auditing, and finance.

In collaboration with our Partners, we deliver solutions in ICT, Engineering, Project Management, Skills Development and Training, Supply and Delivery of materials and components.
